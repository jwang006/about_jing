HEALING JOURNEY

"Healing Journey" is an innovative interior design practice that creates a therapeutic space. This design employs anthropomorphism, to extend interior design into the realms of emotional and mental healing. Inspired by "An Unlikely Pilgrimage of Harold Fry" where self-healing is achieved through walking, "Healing Journey" perceives the space itself as a "doctor," guiding visitors to actively participate, introspect, and undergo an emotional healing process. Instead of focusing on meeting physical needs, "Healing Journey" expands the definition of functionality to address individuals' emotional and mental requirements. It explores the interaction between individuals and their environment and examines the influence of space on personal psychological and emotional states.
