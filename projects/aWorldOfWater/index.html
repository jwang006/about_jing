A WORLD OF WATER

Humans have harnessed water— from leveraging natural rivers for irrigation and farming to depending on it as a daily source of hydration, even drawing upon it as a source of spiritual inspiration— and in return, water nourishes and sustains us. It permeates every aspect of our existence, interwoven into the framework of our daily lives, enveloping our everyday experience much like the spaces we inhabit. "A World of Water" demonstrates the role of water in spatial applications, fostering engagement between occupants and their surroundings, and provoking a re-evaluation of our connection with our immediate environment.
