MEMORY SOUND

Sound Memory is an installation artwork situated within an abandoned building. This work presents a reinterpretation and exploration of environmental sounds, particularly those often overlooked in our daily life. By using the physical properties of sound, this work is recreating the century-long history and evolution of the community. Designed with the theme of "response", sound serves as the medium of historical narratives, intervening in the physical behaviors of humans, compelling spectators to passively encounter and perceive sound during viewers' visit. The interactivity of sound transforms the audience from mere bystanders into participants, enabling them to touch history and experience the ebb and flow of time and space.
