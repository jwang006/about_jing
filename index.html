ABOUT

Jing Wang

Jing is a digital creative artist and designer with a deep focus on everyday life. Continually exploring and questioning the habits and beliefs many take for granted, she uses data and algorithmic simulations to amplify the often overlooked nuances of daily life and the micro-worlds of biology and nature. Through her work, she presents a unique viewpoint, offering a renewed interpretation of the world around us, driven by her curiosity and reflection on life's finer details.
